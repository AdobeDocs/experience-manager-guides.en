# Content Review in AEM Guides

AEM Guides Review workflow lets authors, SMEs, and reviewers collaborate on DITA topics and maps without leaving AEM. It supports annotations, versioning, task management, and multi-stage approvals.

- **Audience** - Customers, partners, and Adobe employees

## Key takeaways

In this session, we covered:
- Managing review as a project (timelines and teams)
- In-context review & annotation, inline accept/reject suggestions
- Multi-reviewer support
- Real time interaction between author and reviewers
- Version-aware review
- Email notifications & reminders
- Review of conditional or reused content
